P.01 — Featured

Real-Time Operational Monitoring System

A complete BI + web system for tracking flights, staffing and overtime at Recife Airport — live, refreshed every 10 minutes.

dnata — Recife Airport

The full story

It started as a Power BI analysis. The data showed that overtime was spiking in ways supervisors couldn't catch until end of shift — too late to do anything about it. The problem wasn't the data. It was the lag.

I built a system that pulls directly from the timesheet system and cross-references it with the operational schedule automatically every 10 minutes. Cloud-hosted on Supabase. Web front-end accessible from any device on the floor. Supervisors went from finding out about overtime problems at month-end to acting on them in real time.

P.02

ANAC → SSIM Flight Schedule Converter

Converted Brazil's public aviation authority data into SSIM format — unlocking 12 airlines that the operations system couldn't previously read.

Najm Award — Emirates Group

The full story

dnata's ground handling system only had SSIM data for GOL, Azul, and LATAM. Every other airline operating in Brazil — 12 of them — had to be managed manually. ANAC publishes all schedule data publicly, but in an incompatible format.

I built a converter that takes the ANAC data and outputs clean, valid SSIM files ready for system import. The project was recognised with the internal Najm award for operational impact.

PythonSSIM formatANAC public dataData modelingAviation ops

P.03

Operational Billing System — Blueground

A field data capture tool that fed directly into a billing dashboard and automated invoice generation — end-to-end.

The full story

Field teams were logging operational events on disconnected spreadsheets, making billing slow and error-prone. There was no single source of truth between what happened on the ground and what got invoiced.

I built an AppSheet application for field data entry, connected it to Supabase, and built a dashboard with a billing view and automated invoice generation. The same data fed a Power BI operational report — connected with attendance records and scheduling information — giving management visibility across the entire operation from one place.
